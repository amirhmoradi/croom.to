Hardware Guide

Build professional video conferencing systems for under $250 per room. Choose the right hardware for your space, from small huddle rooms to large conference rooms.

Platform Flexibility

While this guide focuses on Raspberry Pi for affordability, Croom runs on any Linux system: x86/x64 desktops, ARM single-board computers, mini PCs, or virtual machines. Use any compatible hardware you prefer!

Minimum requirements: 2GB RAM, 8GB storage, ARM or x86 64-bit processor, Linux kernel 5.4+
