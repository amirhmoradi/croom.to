# PiMeet User Guide

## Welcome to PiMeet

PiMeet transforms any conference room with a TV into a smart meeting room. Simply turn on the device, and it will automatically join your next scheduled meeting.

---

## Quick Start

### Starting a Meeting

1. **Turn on the TV** using the power button or remote
2. **Wait for PiMeet to start** (approximately 30 seconds)
3. **Your meeting joins automatically** when it's time

That's it! PiMeet handles everything else.

### During a Meeting

The meeting interface depends on which platform is being used (Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, or Webex), but common controls include:

| Action | How To |
|--------|--------|
| Mute/Unmute | Press the microphone button on screen |
| Camera On/Off | Press the camera button on screen |
| End Meeting | Press the hang up/leave button |
| View Participants | Press the participants icon |

### Ending a Meeting

- Meetings end automatically when all participants leave
- To leave early, press the red "Leave" or "Hang Up" button
- The device returns to idle, ready for the next meeting

## Common Scenarios

### Joining an Ad-hoc Meeting

If you need to join a meeting that's not on the calendar:

1. Use a laptop or phone to add the meeting to the room's calendar
2. PiMeet will detect the new meeting and join automatically
3. Alternatively, contact IT for manual meeting entry

### Changing Meeting Platforms

PiMeet automatically detects which platform to use based on the calendar invite. No action needed from you.

### Sharing Your Screen

To share content in a meeting:

1. **From a laptop:** Use the meeting's built-in screen share feature
2. **Wireless sharing:** Connect to the same network and use AirPlay/Miracast (if enabled)
3. **HDMI cable:** Connect via the room's HDMI input (if available)

### Audio Issues

If you can't hear or be heard:

1. Check if the microphone is muted (look for mute icon)
2. Verify the TV volume is turned up
3. Check that the webcam is properly connected
4. If problems persist, contact IT support

### Video Issues

If the camera isn't working:

1. Check if video is enabled in the meeting
2. Look for a camera indicator light on the webcam
3. Ensure nothing is blocking the camera
4. If problems persist, contact IT support

## Meeting Room Etiquette

### Before the Meeting
- Arrive 2-3 minutes early to ensure the room is ready
- Check that the previous meeting has ended
- Verify your meeting appears on the room's calendar

### During the Meeting
- Speak clearly and at normal volume
- Mute when not speaking in large meetings
- Position yourself in view of the camera
- Avoid walking behind speakers

### After the Meeting
- Ensure you've left the video meeting
- Take your belongings
- Report any issues to IT

## Troubleshooting

### PiMeet Won't Turn On

| Check | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| TV power | Ensure TV is plugged in and turned on |
| HDMI input | Make sure TV is on correct HDMI input |
| Device power | Check that PiMeet device has power (look for lights) |
| Restart | Turn TV off and on again |

### Meeting Didn't Join Automatically

| Possible Cause | Solution |
|----------------|----------|
| Not on calendar | Add meeting to room's calendar |
| Wrong room | Verify you invited the correct room |
| Network issue | Check if TV shows "offline" status |
| Calendar sync | Wait 2-3 minutes for calendar to sync |

### Poor Audio/Video Quality

| Issue | Possible Solution |
|-------|-------------------|
| Choppy video | Other participants may have bandwidth issues |
| Echo | Mute when not speaking |
| Low volume | Increase TV volume |
| Dark video | Ensure room lighting is adequate |

### "Device Offline" Message

1. Wait 2-3 minutes (may be temporary network issue)
2. Check if other devices in the room have network access
3. Contact IT support if issue persists

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How does PiMeet know when I have a meeting?
A: PiMeet monitors the room's calendar. When you invite the room to a meeting, PiMeet sees it and joins automatically at the scheduled time.

### Q: Can I use PiMeet for personal calls?
A: PiMeet is configured for the room's calendar only. For personal calls, use your laptop or phone.

### Q: Why is the camera always on?
A: The camera indicator (if present) shows when the camera is active. It's only used during meetings and is not recording otherwise.

### Q: Can I change the volume?
A: Yes, use the TV remote to adjust volume, or use the volume controls in the meeting interface.

### Q: What happens if my meeting runs over?
A: PiMeet will stay in the meeting until you leave. Be mindful of the next scheduled meeting in the room.

### Q: Can I use PiMeet with my own laptop?
A: PiMeet is designed to work standalone. If you need to share content from your laptop, use screen sharing within the meeting.

### Q: Is my meeting private?
A: Yes. PiMeet only joins meetings on the room's calendar. No data is stored locally after meetings end.

### Q: What meeting platforms work with PiMeet?
A: PiMeet supports Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, and Webex. The platform is detected automatically from your calendar invite.
