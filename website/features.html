Powerful Features for Every Meeting Room

Enterprise-grade video conferencing capabilities without the enterprise price tag.

Multi-Platform Meeting Support

Seamlessly join meetings on Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, and Webex. One device, all platforms.

AI-Powered Auto-Framing

Keep participants perfectly framed with intelligent tracking. Supports Hailo-8L, Coral TPU, and CPU fallback.

Noise Reduction

Crystal-clear audio with AI-powered noise cancellation. Eliminate background noise and echoes automatically.

Speaker Detection & Tracking

Automatically identify and focus on active speakers. Perfect for PTZ camera control and auto-switching.

Calendar Sync

Integrate with Google Calendar and Microsoft 365. Meetings auto-join on schedule with zero intervention.

Touch Screen Interface

Local room control with a beautiful Qt6-based interface. Start meetings, adjust settings, and control displays.

Fleet Management Dashboard

Centralized control for all devices. Monitor, configure, and update hundreds of rooms from one place.

Zero-Touch Provisioning

QR code enrollment, USB configuration, and captive portal setup. Deploy at scale without touching each device.

Meeting Transcription

Self-hosted transcription with Vexa integration. 100+ languages, AI summaries, and searchable archives.

Enterprise Security

End-to-end encryption, RBAC, audit logging. Built for GDPR, SOC 2, and HIPAA compliance.

HDMI-CEC Control

Automatic TV/display control via HDMI-CEC. Turn on displays when meetings start, off when they end.

Analytics & Reporting

Room utilization metrics, meeting analytics, and usage reports. Data-driven insights for your spaces.

Supported Hardware

Runs on affordable hardware you already have or can easily acquire.

Raspberry Pi 5

Recommended for best performance. 4GB+ RAM, supports all AI accelerators.

Raspberry Pi 4

Excellent compatibility. 4GB+ RAM recommended for smooth operation.

x86_64 Linux

Deploy on Intel NUCs, mini PCs, or existing server hardware.

AI Accelerator Support

Hailo-8L

26 TOPS performance. Best value for edge AI features on Raspberry Pi.

Coral TPU

Google's Edge TPU. USB or M.2 versions supported.

CPU Fallback

Works without accelerators. Graceful degradation of AI features.
