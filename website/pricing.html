Frequently Asked Questions

Is Croom really free?

Yes! The Community edition is 100% free and open-source under the MIT license. You can deploy unlimited devices with no per-seat or per-room fees.

What's included in the Pro plan?

Pro adds centralized fleet management, zero-touch provisioning, meeting transcription with AI summaries, analytics dashboards, and priority email support.

Can I self-host everything?

Absolutely. All features including the fleet management dashboard can be self-hosted. Pro and Enterprise plans provide hosted options for convenience.

What hardware do I need?

A Raspberry Pi 4/5, any USB camera or webcam, and optionally an AI accelerator (Hailo-8L or Coral TPU). Total cost: $200-500 per room.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes! Contact us for custom pricing on deployments of 50+ devices. We also offer special rates for educational institutions and non-profits.

What support is available?

Community users get GitHub Issues and community forums. Pro gets email support. Enterprise gets dedicated support channels and SLA guarantees.
